NOT YOURTYPICAL ARTIST

Mike's story is one of continual evolution: from chart-topping pop star, to grieving son on a soul-searching walk, to the mountaintop (literally), and onward into a life of service and self-discovery.

Mike Posner's career has never followed the rules. He's written some of the most ubiquitous songs of the past decade, collaborated with everyone from Justin Bieber to Lil Wayne to Avicii, walked 2,800 miles across America, and summited Mount Everest.

For years, millions have watched him repeatedly risk failure and physical danger in pursuit of something deep and true. What emerged is someone who not only commands a wide, multi-generational audience, but who actually makes a difference in their lives.
